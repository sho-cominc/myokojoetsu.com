Shop

買 / kau

Things worth carrying home from snow country.

What we mean by shop
This isn't a souvenir list. It's a small, slowly-built guide to things that actually come from this region — made by people who still live and work here, with materials that belong to these mountains and this coast.

We write about the maker, the method, and where to find it.
